About Me

My name is Ben Wurster and I am currently a sophomore at the University of Wisconsin-Madison pursuing degrees in Mechanical Engineering, Computer Science, and Applied Mathematics. Making stuff, asking questions, and exploring the fundamental nature of the world around us has always driven me to excel academically and find passion in doing so.

In my free time, I would consider myself to be an extremely active, wholesome-lifestyle person who actively seeks out new experiences and personal growth. Every day is an opportunity to become better and enjoy the limited time one has. Beyond this extensive repository of projects, I seek to maintain an active social life, prioritizing family and friends above all else. They are a source of creative inspiration that tethers me to a hunger for being the best me I can be every day.

As you may have already noticed, most of my personal design projects are focused on highlights of popular culture or improving the failures or shortcomings of existing products. These are deep motivations of mine because they always seem to make those around me happy. We can all connect through popular culture, and we all hate when things fall apart or have the potential to be better. I hope to continue to derive joy from such projects moving into the future as I seek to attain and apply greater knowledge and experience.

Due to my limited exposure to higher level classes while in school, my student projects may appear rather empty. This is not due to a lack of engagement but to the fact that the projects I have done have not been of my own intellectual conception. I will most certainly have more projects occupying that space as I emerge into higher level classes. For the moment though, feel free to note the depth and abundance of my independent projects.

On a final note, if you are reading this page, I want to thank you for taking the time to peruse my passionate pursuits over the years. I have had a lot of fun making many of these projects and it is nice to have them seen by anyone who is interested.
